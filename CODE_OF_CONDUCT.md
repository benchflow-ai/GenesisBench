# Code of Conduct

GenesisBench is committed to a respectful, inclusive, and technically rigorous
community.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Critique ideas, methods, evidence, and code—not people.
- State uncertainty and limitations clearly.
- Respect privacy, credentials, licenses, and responsible disclosure.
- Help contributors reproduce results and improve benchmark validity.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information or credentials.
- Deliberately manipulating benchmark evidence or concealing contamination.
- Repeated disruption of technical discussion or review.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainers through the
organization's established contact channel. Maintainers may edit or remove
content, close discussions, restrict participation, or take other proportionate
action to protect the community.
